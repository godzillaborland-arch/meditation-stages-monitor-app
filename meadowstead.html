How to play

🌱 Pick a seed in the market, then click a brown plot to plant it
💧 Click a growing crop to select it, then water it — watering cuts grow time by a third
✨ Crops glow gold when ripe — click them or use "Harvest all ready"
⭐ Harvests earn coins and XP — level up to unlock better seeds
🔓 Green plots unlock automatically as you level up
🚰 Water refills over time (1 drop every 40s, up to 8) — spend it wisely
📅 Finish the daily task for bonus coins — a fresh one arrives each day
💾 Everything saves automatically, and crops keep growing while you're away
